# Code of Conduct

Thank you for contributing to this repository and helping improve learning resources for the community.

## 1. Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining an open, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone who contributes to or engages with this repository. Our goal is to encourage learning, collaboration, and the sharing of knowledge related to Computer Science fundamentals.

## 2. Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

* Communicate respectfully and professionally
* Provide constructive, relevant, and thoughtful feedback
* Contribute in a manner that improves the quality and clarity of the repository
* Respect diverse perspectives, experiences, and skill levels
* Maintain a positive and collaborative learning environment

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable:

* Harassment, discrimination, or offensive remarks of any kind
* Personal attacks, insults, intimidation, or abusive language
* Submission of spam, irrelevant, or intentionally misleading content
* Plagiarism or failure to provide proper attribution
* Any behavior that disrupts constructive collaboration

## 4. Contribution Standards

To maintain high-quality content:

* Contributions should remain relevant to Computer Science fundamentals
* Content should be clear, concise, accurate, and well-structured
* Formatting and organization should remain consistent throughout the repository
* Proper credit must be given for referenced resources or external materials
* Contributors using AI-assisted tools should review and verify generated content before submission

Beginner-friendly contributions such as typo fixes, formatting improvements, and additional explanations are always welcome.

## 5. Enforcement

Repository maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take appropriate action when violations occur, including:

* Editing or removing contributions
* Issuing warnings for inappropriate behavior
* Temporarily or permanently restricting contributor access

## 6. Reporting Issues

If you experience or observe behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please open an issue or contact the repository maintainer respectfully. All reports will be reviewed fairly and handled with discretion.

## 7. Acknowledgment

By participating in this repository, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct and contribute toward maintaining a positive, professional, and collaborative environment for everyone.
